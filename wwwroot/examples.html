Quality of Teaching

Working closely with the EXFS learnign diaries and using "Starting Points" enables me to evaluate where each child is in their learning and development, guiding me within the areas to reach their goals.

By monitoring their progress (Best Fits) I am able to set appropriate challenges to stretch their imagination.

Children are settled, happy and secure, they respond very well to my guidance when appropriate.

I offer sufficient challenges to further their development emotionally and physically. I ensure they are taken out on a daily basis to enjoy fresh air and explore different settings e.g. "woods", sea wall, play park, toddlers groups.
